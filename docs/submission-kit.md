## Short description (EN, ~160자)

> Abysso is a free, private Mac cleaner. Scan caches and logs with safety badges, find large files with plain-language explanations, and uninstall apps together with their leftovers — everything stays on your device.

## Long description (EN)

> Abysso keeps your Mac light without an account, a subscription, or your data ever leaving your device.
>
> - **Smart Clean** — scans caches, logs, and broken downloads by category, with safety badges that tell you what's safe to delete. Deletions go through the Trash, so they're recoverable.
> - **Large Files** — a size-ranked bar chart of what's eating your disk, with plain-language labels for cryptic files (e.g. "Git repository data").
> - **App Uninstaller** — removes an app together with its leftover caches, settings, and support files; also finds leftovers from apps you deleted long ago.
> - **Delete Completely** — multi-pass secure shredding for sensitive files.
> - **Optimization & Maintenance** — login items, resource-heavy apps, RAM alerts, DNS flush, Spotlight re-index.
> - Live system dashboard, menu bar assistant, auto-updates (Sparkle), 3 languages (English · 한국어 · 日本語).
>
> Free beta for Apple Silicon Macs.
